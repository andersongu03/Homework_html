About this product

Commemorating 35 years of a classic silhouette, the Air Jordan 3 Retro 'White Cement Reimagined' marks the return of an original colorway and duplicating the build of the original 1988 model. The upper combines white leather with overlays on the heel and toe in the trademark elephant print that designer Tinker Hatfield introduced, with Varsity Red pops on the tongue’s Jumpman branding and the lace loops. Additional branding appears on the heel tab with ‘Nike Air.’ Comfort underfoot is ensured by a visible Air-sole unit and the back heel and midsole are imbued with an aged yellow tint to showcase a vintage look.
